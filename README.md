# InkQuest

*Every quest begins with a word.*

InkQuest creates interactive gamebooks for English language learners — branching stories you play in the browser, graded to CEFR levels, with optional vocabulary tips that never get in the way of the story.

No installs, no sign-ups. Pick a story, make your choices, see where they lead.

## How to Play

Open any story page and start reading. At the end of each section, choose what to do next. Your choices — and the skills you pick at the start — shape how the story ends. Tap or hover on highlighted words for vocabulary help, or switch the tips off and just enjoy the ride.

Every story works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

## For Teachers

These gamebooks are free to play with your students in class. If you use them, I'd love to hear how it went.
